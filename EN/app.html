Robot control application

We have created an app in appinventor that allows us to control the robot manually from the mobile. The app allows us to control the robot forwards, backwards, to the left, to the right and to stop, we have added a horn as an innovation. To make it work you have to search for bluetooth devices and connect to the BQ robot.
